The inverse of a partial permutation.

Returns
the inverse of *this. The inverse of \(p\) is the unique partial permutation \(i\) such that \( p * i * p = p\) and \( i * p * i = i\)
Example:
Perm16 x = {0,3,2,4,0xFF,5,6,0xFF,8,9,11,0xFF,12,0xFF,0xFF,0xFF};
x.inverse()
